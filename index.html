Momentous Events in Hans Rosling's Life

  • 27th July 1948 - Born in Uppsala, Sweden
  • 1967-1974 - Studied Statistics and Medicine at Uppsala University
  • 1972 - Studied Public Health at St John's Medical College in Bangalore, India
  • 1976 - Became a licensed physician
  • 1979-1981 - Served as District Medical Officer in Nacala in northern Mozambique
  • 1981 - Began investigating an outbreak of Konzo, a paralytic disease first described in the Democratic Republic of Congo. His investigations earned him a PhD at Upppsala University in 1986
  • 2009 - Listed as one of 100 leading global thinkers by Foreign Policy
  • 2011 - Listed as one of 100 most creative people in business by Fast Company
  • 2011 - Elected member of the Swedish Academy of Engineering Sciences
  • 2012 - Elected member of the Swedish Academy of Sciences
  • 2012 - Included in the Time 100 list of the world's 100 most influential people
  • 7th February 2017 - A year after being diagnosed with Pancreatic Cancer, Rosling died in Sweden, at the age of 68
